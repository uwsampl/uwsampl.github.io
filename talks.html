--- layout: default title: SAMPL Lunch Talks --- SAMPL organizes group lunch on a weekly basis and we invite speakers from both academia and industry to present their work. The goal is to provide a platform for researchers to share their work and to foster collaborations. The talks are open to everyone in the community. If you are interested in giving a talk, please contact the organizers.
